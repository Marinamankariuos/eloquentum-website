Common Questions

How does the 7-day trial work?

New accounts start with 7 days of Pro access through Eloquentum's server-side trial. The trial does not require a card. Paid subscriptions and in-app purchases use Apple's App Store purchase flow.

How are daily exercises chosen?

Eloquentum uses diagnostic scans, practice evidence, mistakes, review history, Practice Focus, and learner context to choose Daily Practice targets. A brand-new account should not show old scan history or progress from another account.

What is Practice Focus?

Practice Focus is the learner-selected mode for General English, IELTS®, or TOEFL® practice. It is not an official exam product and does not guarantee an official score.

What should I trust in Progress?

Progress screens should reflect stored scan or practice evidence. Fresh accounts should use honest empty states until the learner completes real practice or diagnostic work.

Which AI providers does the app use?

Gemini is the primary text-AI provider. OpenAI may be used for configured fallback/secondary text AI and Whisper-1 transcription. Azure Speech may be used only for optional Speech Coach pronunciation assessment when enabled and consented.
